About Us


Tradition since 2020

It all started in 2020, when a group of four girls met at a SheHacks Hackathon. Since then, they have become renowned food critics travelling all over London and Toronto trying new dishes. Their names are Emily, Sonia, Ivy, and Kelly -- Western University students of different backgrounds. They specialize in Asian cuisine and noodles, but they enjoy all types of foods.
